# Email Signature — Privacy Footer

When clients send us a brief by email, we collect their personal
information (their name, contact, the personal info of any people
mentioned in the brief). APP 5 says we should notify them or ensure
they're aware of how we'll handle that information at or before the
time of collection.

The cleanest way to do that for an inbound email channel is a small
privacy footer in our standard outbound email signature, so every reply
includes a pointer to the policy.

---

## Recommended footer

Add this to Jasper's, Jemma's, and any other Saunders & Co team
member's Gmail signature (Gmail → Settings → General → Signature):

```
Jasper Bailey
Saunders & Co Agency · Photography Production
+61 ___ ___ ___ · jasper@saundersandco.com.au · saundersandco.com.au

Privacy: by emailing us you may share personal information about
yourself or others. We handle that information per our privacy policy
at saundersandco.com.au/privacy. To request access, correction, or
deletion of your data, email privacy@saundersandco.com.au.
```

---

## Why this works as an APP 5 notice

APP 5.6 (Examples of reasonable steps) explicitly contemplates "any
subsequent electronic or paper-based communication" as a vehicle for
the notice when realtime notification at point of collection isn't
practical. Email is the canonical example.

The footer doesn't have to repeat the full APP 5.2 matters — it just
has to point to where they're available. The link to `/privacy` is the
operative part.

---

## How to set it in Gmail

1. Open Gmail
2. Settings (gear icon) → See all settings
3. General tab → scroll to "Signature"
4. Either edit the existing signature or create a new one
5. Paste the block above, replacing the placeholders with your real
   contact details
6. Set as default for "On reply/forward use my signature" too — so the
   notice goes on every reply, not just new emails
7. Save changes at the bottom of the page

Repeat for any team member whose Gmail account is used for
client-facing correspondence.

## Optional: shorter version

If you want a leaner signature, the bare minimum that still meets the
spirit of APP 5 is:

```
Privacy: saundersandco.com.au/privacy · privacy@saundersandco.com.au
```

This is enough because it directs the recipient to the full APP 5.2
matters in our published policy.
